============================================================
State Pattern - Music Player Example
============================================================

1. Click Play (Stopped -> Playing)
Playing: Track 1

2. Click Next (Playing -> Next Track)
Next track: Track 2

3. Click Lock (Playing -> Locked)
Locked (while playing)

4. Click Play (Locked -> Do nothing)
Locked... Do nothing.

5. Click Lock (Locked -> Playing)
Unlocked -> Playing

6. Click Play (Playing -> Stopped)
Playback stopped.
Paused.

============================================================
Key Benefit: Object behavior changes based on its internal state,
avoiding massive if-else statements!
============================================================
