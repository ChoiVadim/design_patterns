Channel 'TechDaily': Attached a new subscriber.

Channel 'TechDaily' is uploading video: 'Python Design Patterns Tutorial'

  [Notification] Hey Alice, TechDaily just uploaded 'Python Design Patterns Tutorial'!

============================================================
Key Benefit: The channel doesn't need to know WHO subscribers are,
just that they implement the Observer interface!
